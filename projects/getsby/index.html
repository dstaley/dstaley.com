--- layout: page title: Gets by ---

gets by is a "collection of stories about living with a mental illness, as told by the tech community". When my good friend Paddy Foran approached me about doing the design for gets by, he didn't even need to say please. I enthusiastically agreed to give gets by the simple, beautiful, typographically-driven design the people sharing their stories deserved.

The stories on gets by are completely unedited; we do not screen interviewees, nor do we modify their stories in any way. Paddy put it this way in his blog post about the launch:

Everything of value came from other people. The credit is theirs, not mine. I'm just the custodian, keeping things running.

Using Bootstrap as a base, the design of gets by utilizes very simple elements to build an interface that looks great on any device. My favorite feature of the design is the alternating colors, which serve to create a distinction between the various heroes who've shared their stories and the section in their interviews.

Another great thing about gets by is that it's open source. When we say anyone can share their story, we mean anyone. Simply send a pull request to GitHub repo and your story will appear on the site, giving a whole new meaning to the term "open".
